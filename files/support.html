Quick Answers

How long does building take?
Standard builds take 5–7 business days. Custom or flagship builds may require 10–14 business days due to cable sleeving and full quality assurance testing.
What's covered under warranty?
All Elysian builds include a 3-year comprehensive warranty covering all components, labor, and shipping for any defect-related issues.
Can I upgrade later?
Absolutely. Elysian owners receive lifetime upgrade consultations and discounted upgrade services. Submit a custom build inquiry through this form.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes. We ship worldwide via insured freight carriers. International orders include full customs documentation and a dedicated export specialist.
